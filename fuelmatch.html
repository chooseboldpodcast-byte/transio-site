What Trasio FuelMatch does for you.

FuelMatch checks every fuel delivery — and when something is wrong, our team works the problem with your vendor so you don't have to.

Step 1

Your store scans the BOL

Staff snaps a photo of the Bill of Lading in the Trasio app at delivery. FuelMatch reads the BOL number, date, supplier, and gallons by grade.

Step 2

Trasio checks the invoice

We compare the vendor invoice — gallons and totals — against the BOL. If everything matches, the delivery quietly moves to reconciled.

Step 3

We work the problem — not you

When something doesn't match, FuelMatch opens a case and our team takes it to the vendor. You see which loads are at risk, how much is on the line, and where each case stands.

Protect your margin

The charges FuelMatch catches before you pay.

Phantom delivery

Billed for fuel that never came

You're invoiced for a load with no BOL on file. Example: 8,800 gallons of 87 at $4.50/gal = $39,600 you should not pay.

Volume overbilling

Invoiced for more than was dropped

The BOL shows fewer gallons than the invoice. Example: BOL says 8,000 gallons, invoice says 8,800 — 800 extra gallons at $4.50/gal = $3,600.
