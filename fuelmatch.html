Every load passes through the truck, the BOL, the invoice, and the bank draft. Almost nobody checks every line before the money moves.

How it works

What FuelMatch does for you.

FuelMatch checks every fuel delivery against the invoice. When something is wrong, Trasio opens the case, tracks the dollars at risk, and works the issue with your vendor so your team does not have to.

Step 1

Your store scans the BOL

Staff photograph the Bill of Lading in the Trasio app at delivery. FuelMatch reads the key delivery details from the BOL.

Step 2

Trasio checks the invoice

We compare the vendor invoice against the BOL. If everything matches, no further action is needed.

Step 3

We work the problem — not you

If something does not match, FuelMatch flags the discrepancy and our team takes it forward with the vendor. You see which deliveries are at risk, how much is on the line, and where each case stands.
